VAPT & vCISO Services

VAPT (Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing)

Our comprehensive Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT) services are designed to identify, assess, and address security risks across your IT infrastructure. Combining automated tools with expert manual testing, we help strengthen your cybersecurity posture and meet regulatory compliance.

  • Network Vulnerability Assessment
    Scan internal and external networks to uncover misconfigurations and weaknesses.
  • Web Application Testing
    Identify and exploit flaws in web apps, including OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities.
  • Infrastructure Security Testing
    Evaluate servers, endpoints, and cloud infrastructure for security gaps.
  • Social Engineering Assessment
    Test human factors with phishing simulations and awareness checks.
  • Wireless Network Testing
    Detect vulnerabilities in Wi-Fi networks and prevent unauthorized access.
  • Compliance-Driven Assessments
    Align security controls with standards like ISO 27001, PCI DSS, and HIPAA.
